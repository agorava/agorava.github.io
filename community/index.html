Contacting Agorava Team

You can contact the Agorava team to get support, send us feedback or propose a contribution. To join us you have multiple solution

Mailing List

Our Mailing list is hosted by Google Group, you can subscribe by sending an empty mail to agorava-dev+subscribe@googlegroups.com or you can join our Google Group (same content as mailing list)

Bintray

Check out Agorava on Bintray.

Issue Tracker

Post your issues, enhancement wishes or ideas on GitHub or JIRA. But please check with us on the mailing list before posting a bug.

Twitter

To stay informed : You can follow us on twitter : @agoravaproj.

IRC

Join us on IRC on irc.freenode.net on the #agorava channel. If you don’t have an IRC client are behind a firewall or are in hurry, you can use kiwiirc (you’ll only have to provide a pseudo)
